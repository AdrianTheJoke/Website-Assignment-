Lesson Overview

We offer a range of networking lessons that help students build a strong foundation in the field.

Lesson 1: Introduction to Networking

Learn the basics of networking, including types of networks, devices, and protocols.

This lesson introduces the core concepts of networking, including how networks are structured and how data flows between devices.

Lesson 2: IP Addressing

Understand IP addressing, including IPv4 and IPv6 formats and how they are used in networks.

In this lesson, you'll explore IP addresses, subnetting, and how IP addresses are assigned in real-world networks.

Lesson 3: Protocols

Learn about different network protocols and how they enable communication over the internet.

This lesson covers the various protocols like TCP/IP, DNS, and HTTP that make the internet function smoothly and securely.
